Our Story

In an age of digital information overload, distinguishing fact from fiction has never been more challenging. Misinformation spreads rapidly, influencing opinions and shaping narratives. VeriScanX was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to create an accessible, reliable tool that empowers individuals to verify the news they consume. Our journey began with a commitment to leveraging technology for good, building an AI that can cut through the noise and provide clarity.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower people with true news and encourage skepticism towards unverified information found on social media. We created this application to provide a quick and easy way to check the facts, fostering a more informed and discerning public. We believe that access to reliable information is a cornerstone of a healthy society.
